## Blog Configuration

The Blog Configuration section lets you manage your directory's blog settings.

### Enable/Disable Blog

<Card title="Blog Toggle" icon="toggle-on">
You can enable or disable the blog on your website at any time.

**When enabled**: Blog is accessible to visitors on your directory
**When disabled**: Blog is completely hidden from your directory
</Card>

### Featured Blog Posts Section

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Featured Section">
    Toggle on the option to show a Featured Blog Posts section on your blog page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Custom Heading">
    Enter a custom heading for the featured section, such as "Featured Articles" or any text you prefer.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save Changes** to apply your blog configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
The featured section will display blog posts that you mark as "Featured" when creating or editing them.
</Info>

## Blog Categories

The Categories section lets you manage blog categories for your directory.

### Creating a Category

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Category">
    Open the category creation form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Category Name">
    Type the name for your new category.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Category">
    Save to create the category.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Categories help organize your blog posts and make it easier for visitors to find content.
</Tip>

## Blog Posts

The Blog Posts section lets you manage all blog posts for your directory.

### Blog Posts Overview

<Card title="Posts Management" icon="newspaper">
**Actions available**:
- Click **Add New** to create a new post
- Use the filter to find posts by:
  - Title
  - Category
  - Author
  - Date

- Title

- Featured setting

**Post actions**:

## Creating a Blog Post

Click **Add New** to create a new blog post. You'll see a form where you can fill in all the details.

### Featured Image

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upload from Computer">
    Upload an image file directly from your computer.
    
    This image will appear at the top of your blog post.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Use Image URL">
    Enter an image URL to use an image hosted elsewhere.
    
    The image will be loaded from the URL you provide.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Title and Description

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Title" icon="heading">
    Enter your blog post title.
    
    Make sure your title is clear and describes what your post is about.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Description" icon="align-left">
    Add a description that gives a good summary of your post.
    
    This helps readers understand what they'll learn from your article.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Author Name

<Card title="Post Author" icon="user">
The Author Name is automatically set to your account (for example, "Zakariae"), but you can change it if needed.

This is useful when you want to:
- Attribute posts to different team members
- Use a different author name
- Credit guest contributors
</Card>

### Categories

<Card title="Organize Your Post" icon="folder">
Select Categories to organize your posts and help readers find them easily.

You can choose one or multiple categories that best fit your post's content.
</Card>

### Content Section

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Markdown Editor">
    Write your post using Markdown syntax.
    
    **Markdown** is a lightweight markup language that lets you format text using simple characters.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="WYSIWYG Editor">
    Use the visual editor to write and format your content.
    
    **WYSIWYG** (What You See Is What You Get) lets you format text visually, similar to a word processor.
    
    **Features**:
    - Format your text
    - Add images
    - Insert links
    - And more formatting options
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### SEO Manager

<Card title="Optimize Your Post" icon="magnifying-glass">
On the right side, you'll see the **SEO Manager**, which shows how well your post is optimized for search engines.

Try to improve your SEO score to get better visibility in search results.
</Card>

### Publishing Options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Published Status" icon="eye">
    Choose if you want your post to be **Published**, making it visible to everyone.
    
    **Published**: Post is live and public
    **Draft**: Post is saved but not visible
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Featured Status" icon="star">
    Choose if you want your post to be **Featured** on your blog.
    
    Featured posts appear in the Featured Blog Posts section on your blog page.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Create Your Post

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Your Content">
    Make sure all fields are filled in and your content looks good.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Settings">
    Verify your published and featured status settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Create">
    Click **Create** to publish your new blog post.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
Your blog post is now live and ready for your audience!
</Check>

## Ads Configuration

The Ads Configuration section allows you to manage every advertising option available on your directory.

### Basic Ads Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate Advertisements">
    Enable the advertising system for your directory.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Section Headline">
    Add a headline for your ads section, such as "Our Partners".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Tagline">
    Include a short tagline like "Interested in advertising here?".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Settings">
    Apply your changes to activate the advertising system.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Display Settings

The Display Settings area gives you full control over where ads appear.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Homepage Ads" icon="house">
    You can show horizontal or vertical ads on the homepage.
    
    Choose the ad type and enable homepage display.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Listing Page Ads" icon="file">
    You can show horizontal or vertical ads on listing pages.
    
    Select your preferred ad format for listing pages.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
You have flexibility to display different ad formats in different locations depending on your needs.
</Info>

## Advertisement Plans

Create advertising packages that businesses can purchase.

### Creating an Advertisement Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Plans">
    Go to the Advertisement Plans section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in Plan Details">
    Enter all the information for the package you want to offer.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Plan">
    Click **Add Plan** to save the package.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Plan Configuration Fields

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Plan Name" icon="heading">
    Name the plan (for example, "Standard Ad").
    
    This is how the plan will be identified in your system.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Display Label" icon="tag">
    Set the display label (for example, "Standard").
    
    This is what advertisers will see when viewing plan options.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Description" icon="align-left">
    Write a short description (for example, "Standard advertising package").
    
    Explain what this advertising plan includes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Price" icon="dollar-sign">
    Set the price for this plan.
    
    You can set:
    - A one-time price (for example, ninety-nine dollars)
    - A monthly recurring price
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Button Label" icon="mouse-pointer">
    Define the button label (for example, "Purchase").
    
    This text will appear on the button advertisers click to buy the plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Featured Plan" icon="star">
    Mark the plan as featured if you want it to stand out.
    
    Featured plans are highlighted to attract more attention.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Benefits Included" icon="list-check">
    List the benefits included in this plan.
    
    **Examples**:
    - "Featured for thirty days"
    - "Top listing position"
    - Any other benefits you want to offer
    
    This helps advertisers understand what they're getting.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Example Advertisement Plan

```plaintext
Plan Name: Standard Ad
Display Label: Standard
Description: Standard advertising package
Price: $99 (or monthly price)
Button Label: Purchase
Featured: Yes/No
Benefits:
- Featured for thirty days
- Top listing position
```

## Ad Spots

The Ad Spots section lets you manage individual advertisement placements.

### Creating an Ad Spot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add New Ad Spot">
    Start creating your first advertisement.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Required Information">
    Fill in all the details for the ad spot.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Ad Spot">
    Click **Create Ad Spot** to add the advertisement to your directory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Ad Spot Configuration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Title" icon="heading">
    Add a title for the ad.
    
    This helps you identify and manage the ad in your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Description" icon="text">
    Add a description for the ad.
    
    Include any notes or details about this advertisement.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Link URL" icon="link">
    Provide the link URL where users will be redirected when they click the ad.
    
    Make sure the link is correct and working.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Ad Image" icon="image">
    Upload the ad image.
    
    **Recommended size for vertical ads**: 300 x 400 pixels
    
    Make sure your image is the correct dimensions for the best display.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Active Status" icon="toggle-on">
    Set the ad to active so it will be displayed on your directory.
    
    **Active**: Ad is visible to visitors
    **Inactive**: Ad is hidden
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Display Order" icon="arrow-up-1-9">
    Choose the display order for the ad.
    
    **Lower numbers appear first**. For example:
    - Order 1 = First position
    - Order 2 = Second position
    - Order 3 = Third position
    
    This controls which ads appear in priority positions.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Ad Type" icon="rectangle-ad">
    Select the ad type.
    
    **Options**:
    - Horizontal ad
    - Vertical ad
    
    Choose based on where and how you want the ad to appear.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Display Location" icon="location-dot">
    Choose the display location.
    
    **Options**:
    - Homepage
    - Listing pages
    - Both
    
    This determines where visitors will see this advertisement.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Complete Ad Spot Example

```plaintext
Title: Partner Company Advertisement
Description: Banner ad for partner company
Link URL: https://example.com
Ad Image: Upload 300x400px image
Active: Yes
Display Order: 1
Ad Type: Vertical
Display Location: Homepage
```

<Check>
Once everything is ready, click **Create Ad Spot** to add the advertisement to your directory.
</Check>
